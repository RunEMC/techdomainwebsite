Lost Something?

Look No Further

Have you ever lost something important and wished there was something that can notify you if someone found it? Or maybe you found something that someone has lost and want to return it to them, but you have no idea how? With the UniFind app, you will be able to enter an item that you have lost and find close matches of similar items found by people nearby.

Are You An U Of T Student?

Localised

If you are a student at the University of Toronto, then this is the right app for you. Currently, this app is only available for the students of the University of Toronto, making it perfecting for finding your lost items around campus.
